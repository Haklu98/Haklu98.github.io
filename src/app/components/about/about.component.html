About Me

Hello dear friend! I am Aleksi, a 26-year old goofy and quite charming young man. I was born and raised in Southern Ostrobothnia, Finland, but I moved to Jyväskylä because of my studies. I always had a certain knick for computers and music, which is why I am on the path that I am right now!

I have studied music ever since I was 6-years old, which means that I've been doing it for 20 years already. On the other hand, I was introduced to computers and videogames at the age of 3 and that's probably why I have such a great interest towards videogame music.

In my free time I tend to play videogames or compose music, or just enjoy different soundtracks and sound engineering in general. I am a part of a band called "Moffat" and I also do the live mixing for the band "Nelosolut". On top of those, I also produce my own music and occasionally do commissions for other people needing music for their own projects.

Nonetheless, I hope you enjoy my work and should the stars be aligned in such way, maybe we will work together!
